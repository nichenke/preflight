### Warning: ADR-R02
**Violation:** The Decision Outcome section uses passive framing ("Chosen option: **PostgreSQL**, because...") rather than active voice. MADR 4.0 and ADR-R02 require the decision statement to use active voice (e.g., "We will use PostgreSQL..." or "We chose PostgreSQL...").
**Fix:** Rewrite the decision statement as: "We will use **PostgreSQL** as the primary data store, because it satisfies all decision drivers — strongest analytics query support, full ACID compliance, and best fit for team expertise."

### Warning: UNIV-04
**Violation:** "strong SQL expertise" in the Decision Drivers section uses the vague adjective "strong" without quantification. Similarly, "excellent analytics support" in the PostgreSQL pros/cons uses "excellent" without a measurable benchmark.
**Fix:** Quantify "strong SQL expertise" (e.g., "4 of 5 backend engineers have 3+ years of production SQL experience") and replace "excellent analytics support" with a concrete capability statement (e.g., "supports window functions, CTEs, and lateral joins natively").
